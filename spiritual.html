15 Day Yoga Plan

As a long time practitioner of yoga, I am an ardent supporter of yoga to release stress and increase life longevity. Here is my 15 day yoga plan everyone should follow:

Day 1: Introduction to Basic Poses

  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
  • Downward Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  • Child's Pose (Balasana)
  • Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)

Day 2: Standing Poses

  • Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)
  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)
  • Extended Triangle Pose (Utthita Trikonasana)
  • Tree Pose (Vrksasana)

Day 3: Seated Poses and Forward Bends

  • Staff Pose (Dandasana)
  • Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)
  • Butterfly Pose (Baddha Konasana)
  • Head-to-Knee Forward Bend (Janu Sirsasana)

Day 4: Gentle Flow

Combine poses from Days 1-3 in a gentle flowing sequence.

Day 5: Hip Openers

  • Pigeon Pose (Eka Pada Rajakapotasana)
  • Cow Face Pose (Gomukhasana)
  • Frog Pose (Mandukasana)

Day 6: Balancing Poses

  • Half Moon Pose (Ardha Chandrasana)
  • Dancer's Pose (Natarajasana)
  • Eagle Pose (Garudasana)

Day 7: Restorative Yoga

  • Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose (Viparita Karani)
  • Reclining Bound Angle Pose (Supta Baddha Konasana)
  • Corpse Pose (Savasana)

Day 8: Core Strengthening

  • Boat Pose (Navasana)
  • Plank Pose
  • Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

Day 9: Twists

  • Seated Twist (Ardha Matsyendrasana)
  • Supine Twist
  • Revolved Triangle Pose (Parivrtta Trikonasana)

Day 10: Backbends

  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)
  • Camel Pose (Ustrasana)
  • Locust Pose (Salabhasana)

Day 11: Strength Building

  • Chair Pose (Utkatasana)
  • Warrior III (Virabhadrasana III)
  • Side Plank Pose (Vasisthasana)

Day 12: Sun Salutations

Practice Sun Salutation A (Surya Namaskar A) series.

Day 13: Mindfulness and Breath Work

Practice deep breathing (Pranayama). Gentle seated and standing poses with focus on breath.

Day 14: Integrating Movement and Breath

Vinyasa flow incorporating poses from previous days.

Day 15: Full Practice

A full yoga session combining all elements from the previous days.
